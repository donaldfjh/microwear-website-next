## ⚠️ Red Flags to Avoid

### 🚩 Warning Signs

1. **No physical factory address** — Likely a trading company
2. **Cannot provide certificates** — Compliance risk
3. **Prices too good to be true** — Quality shortcuts
4. **No sample process** — Production risk
5. **Only accepts Western Union** — Scam indicator

### ✅ Green Flags

1. Factory tour available (virtual or in-person)
2. Third-party audit reports
3. Referenceable clients in your region
4. Clear MOQ and pricing tiers
5. Responsive communication (<24h)
